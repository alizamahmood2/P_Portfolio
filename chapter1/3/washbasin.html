Chapter 1: Industrial Design / Washbasin Unit

Collaborative Project
Students UG Award Winner at National
Furniture Design Competition, Turkey '17
Furniture, Installation
Nov - Dec 2016

A self-cleaning washbasin unit for primary school bathrooms in Turkey.

The washbasin decreases the amount of water consumed while using and cleaning it. It comes in a series of colors with accessories that encourage better hygiene habits for children.

Left: Closed for Cleaning; Center: Open; Right: Internal Piping

It can be operated by the janitor after school breaks. The lid closes and locks with silicon sealing by inserting a key in the pivot. Then, the 3-minute cleaning cycle starts, that uses minimal water and cleaning liquid due to maximum pressurized coverage provided by the piping in the lid. Parts can be disassembled and replaced.




This was one of the best teams I've had the pleasure of working with. I produced the product renders and technical drawings displayed here. The clay model was made by our team mate who had previous experience with clay.


Made in collaboration with Ibrahim Elshamy, Serhat Ünlü and Başak Laçin.

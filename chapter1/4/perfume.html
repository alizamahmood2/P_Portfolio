Chapter 1: Industrial Design / Perfume Bottle

Solo Project
Islamabad, Pakistan
Product, Luxury
April - May 2016

A beautiful and light perfume scent bottled in a unique form designed to be easily held in a hand.

It was the middle of spring and I (an asthmatic person) serendipitously happened upon a place in Islamabad that was densely populated with eucalyptus trees. The first breath of fresh air that I inhaled, felt as though it cleared up my enitre respitory system after a month of feeling like my windpipe was constricted and thin - this moment of relief and joy is personal yet relatable, and its the experience that I wanted to bottle.

This bottle is inspired by the mechanism of an asthma inhaler, but it breaks away from the medicinal aspect and feels elegant, luxurious and unique. The spray mechanism is the combination of an aerosol spray and asthma inhaler pump technology.

Model of bottle hand carved out of a platster of paris block.

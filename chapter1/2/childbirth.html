Chapter 1: Industrial Design / Van for Childbirth, 'حفاظت سے'
                   translates to ‘with care/protection’

Colaborative Project
Islamabad, Pakistan
Vehicle Interior, Service
Nov - Dec 2017

Approximately 1 woman dies every 20 minutes in Pakistan due to childbirth complications.

This service provides care to mother and child at time of labor/childbirth in rural areas of Balochistan, Pakistan.





Research:

Balochistan has an alarmingly high maternal mortality rate. It's rural areas have a traditional system of midwives but they are not trained to deal with any problems that may arise during a home birth. There is a lack of proper facilities closeby, and lack of transportation to facilities in nearby cities.

This project redesigns the back of a Toyota Hilux vehicle to convert it into a portable safe space for childbirth and related pre and post services.

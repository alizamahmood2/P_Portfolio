Hi, I'm Aliza. I'm a Pakistani industrial designer based in New York City.

I graduated from Parsons School of Design with an MFA in Design and Technology in May 2020. Prior to that, I got my Bachelors in Industrial Design from National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST) from Pakistan, while spending a year of that degree studying abroad on scholarship at Middle East Technical University (METU) in Turkey.

My work ranges from industrial designs to interactive installations to critical design projects. Most recently, I've been interested in tactile technology, critical design, and progressive industrial deisgn practises.

I’ve grown up in multiple cities of Pakistan. I’m bilingual and always excited to learn new languages. Other than the realms of design and art, I love alternative rock music, chai, dogs, and astrophysics.
